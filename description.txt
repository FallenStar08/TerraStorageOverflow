A Quality of Life expansion for the TerraStorage mod.

FEATURES:
- Smart Overflow: Items go to storage if your inventory is full (Void Bag style).
- Multiple Networks: Automatically cycles through all bound remotes in your inventory.
- Left-Click to Open: Use your Remote Terminal like a normal item.
- Shift-Click Redirect: Shift-clicking from chests bypasses full inventories.
- Loot All Support: Excess items from chests fly directly into your disks.
- Live Tooltips: See disk capacity and coordinates on the item tooltip.
- Multiplayer Optimized: Batches deposits to prevent server lag.

Requires TerraStorage (Base Mod) to function.
