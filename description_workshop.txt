[h1]TerraStorage Overflow[/h1]

A small addon for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3687137546][b]TerraStorage[/b][/url]. Instead of leaving items on the ground or manually clicking through menus, this mod turns your Remote Terminal into a smart vacuum for your storage network.

[h2]🛠 Features[/h2]

[list]
[*] [b]Smart Overflow:[/b] Automatically redirects picked-up items to your storage disks if your inventory is full, similar to what the void bag does. 
[*] [b]Multi-Network Support:[/b] The mod intelligently cycles through all bound networks until the item finds a home.
[*] [b]Improved Interaction:[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Left-Click to Open:[/b] No more middle-clicking in the inventory. Use the remote like a normal item to open the UI.
[*] [b]Shift-Click Redirect:[/b] Shift-clicking from chests will bypass your inventory and go straight to the network if you're out of space.
[*] [b]Loot All Integration:[/b] Hit "Loot All" on a chest and watch the excess fly directly into your network.
[/list]
[*] [b]Live Tooltips:[/b] Hover over a remote to see its bound coordinates and a real-time capacity readout (Used / Total).
[*] [b]Multiplayer Optimized:[/b] Uses a custom buffering system to batch item deposits. This prevents packet-spam and should keep the server smooth even during mass-looting, probably.
[/list]

[h2]Performance[/h2]
Should be fine? I use event driven caching instead of constant pooling.
